Cognitive Benefits

Help yourself be likeable!

There are many benefits to one's emotion for sleeping. This isn't just any kind of sleep but sleep that is at night and lasts 8 hours.

Memory benefits

Sleeping for 8 hours a night helps to improve your memory. Memory is split into three different types: Procedural, Visual, and Declarative.

Emotional benefits

Studies show that sleeping more helps ones emotion. If you have ever noticed that when you are tired, you get agitated more easily, you have experienced the drawback of sleep deprivation on your emotions. Essentially, when you are sleepy, your brain cannot communicate effectively. The particular parts of your brain that play a role in your emotions are the amygdala and the medial frontal cortex.
